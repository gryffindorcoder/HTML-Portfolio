The Best Movies According to Jeremy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Harry Potter lears that he is a boy wizard.
He has to learn how to use magic to protect the magical world.
Harry learns that Lord Voldemort is trying to eliminate him to let darkness rule and reign the magical world.

The Rookie

I love The Rookie because its about baseball.
Jimmy Morris is a High School coach that challenges his players to step it up.
In return he will try out for the majors.
He gets a surprise when he challenges his players.

The Natural

I love the movie The Natural because it is about a baseball player that defies the odds.
Roy Hobbs plays in the majors and people want him to take a dive.
He has to make the sacrifice to be able to play the game of baseball for cheaters or give it up.
